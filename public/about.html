About Always Carbon

Founded to address Australia's carbon removal challenge through world-class engineering expertise and innovative biochar technology.

Our Mission

Always Carbon was founded to scale biochar technology globally and address Australia's massive carbon removal challenge. We believe in permanent solutions that create value for all stakeholders.

Our track record includes delivering major projects like the Kangaroo Island initiative, described as the "largest biochar project in the world," demonstrating our ability to execute at scale.
